

## Stop and Go Operation (Level 1 Roads)


A Stop and Go operation is suitable for **Level 1** roads with a speed limit up to **60 km/h**.

- Maximum site length:**500 m**



### 🔹 Staffing and Operation
- A **single TTM operator** may be used for works **up to 200 m**.  
- For lengths **over 100 m**, **two automatic Stop/Go signs** must be used.  
- **Automated Stop and Go disc boards** are recommended when **only one operator** is available.



### 🔹 Layout Requirements
- Taper angle:**45°** 
- Cone spacing:**1 m**(at taper)  



### 🔹 Traffic Volume Check
Before starting operations, conduct a **3-minute traffic count** in the busiest direction:
- Proceed only if **< 20 vehicles** are counted in 3 minutes.  


### 🔹 Cone
- 10m cone <= 60km/h
- 20m cone > 60km/h
