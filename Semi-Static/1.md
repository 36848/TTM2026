## Semi Static Operation (SSO) 半静态作业



### Definition 

Works where the operations are mobile or make short-duration stops continuously along a road, using static warning signs.

指作业具有移动性，或沿道路连续进行短时间停车作业，并使用固定式警告标志。

Only suitable for **Level 1 & 2** roads  

不受交通流量限制/不受天气条件限制


### Visibility Conditions : Good  


### Stop Duration Limit | 停车时间限制 < 15minutes
